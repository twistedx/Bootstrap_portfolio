About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio page. My name is Sean Gowing. I have a multitude of life experience and decided that web development was going to be my next new frontier

Prior to doing web development I was a US Marine for 7 years. I did one deployment to Iraq in 2004 in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom. One thing I can say about Iraq was "It's hot!!!!" The hottest day, while we were there, was 135 degrees

After getting out of the Marine Corps I was a Police Officer for 15 years. Due to a back injury, I was forced to retire at 38 years old. I had the pleasure of working in jobs like narcotics, SWAT, SWAT Sniper, Bicycle Patrol, and fugitive apprehension team. I have worked with other agencies like DEA, ATF, ABC, and many others. I have spent a lot of time in the courtroom testifying in front of many juries for a multitude of crimes such as murder, child endangerment as well as narcotic related offenses as well as gang crimes.

After retiring from the Department, This brought me to my next new career in programming and web development. I really enjoy working with computers and have been using since 386's came out.

This portfolio will be a work in progress for me and I hope you enjoy seeing the projects that I am working on.
